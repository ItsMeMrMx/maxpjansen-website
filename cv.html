I am a research fellow at the Chair for Sociology with a Focus on Empirical Educational Research at Goethe University Frankfurt and the Institute for Empirical Analytical Research (InFER).

I have been part of the Research Group 'Reconfiguration and Internalisation of Social Structure'(RISS)since February 2022. I hold a Master of Arts in International Studies, Peace and Conflict Research and Gender Studies from the Faculty of Social Sciences at Goethe University Frankfurt, as well as a Bachelor of Science in Social Sciences from the Faculty of Management, Economics and Social Sciences at the University of Cologne. In the past, I studied and conducted research at Humboldt University (Germany), Tel Aviv University (Israel), the American University of Beirut (Lebanon), Ben-Gurion University of the Negev (Israel), and Corvinus University of Budapest (Hungary).
